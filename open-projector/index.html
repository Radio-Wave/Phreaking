Open Projector

The monthly meetup for computational artists

A show and tell for visual artists, researchers, and curious minds to share work, experiments, works in progress, poems, stories, ideas, and anything else!

Running on the last Tuesday of every month at Social in Copeland Park, Open Projector is a casual meetup to meet new people and learn new things.

Whether you have a finished project or just a sketch of an idea, we welcome you to come and share it with the community.

Join us for an evening of creativity and inspiration!
