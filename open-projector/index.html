Open Projector

The monthly meetup for computational artists

Show and tell for visual artists, come show your work, experiments, works in progress, poems, stories, ideas, and anything else!

Running on the last Tuesday of every month, Open Projector is a casual meetup for computational artists to share their work and ideas. Whether you have a finished project or just a sketch of an idea, we welcome you to come and share it with the community. Each month, we gather at Social in Copeland Park to connect, collaborate, and inspire each other.
Join us for an evening of creativity and insperation!
